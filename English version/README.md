## Project Brief

**VC HB3 Accelerator** is a venture fund and software provider creating a global asset tokenization ecosystem through the **Digital Legal Entity (DLE)** platform.

### Key Features

- ‚úÖ **Production-ready tokenization platform** with AI assistant
- ‚úÖ **Acceleration program** for entrepreneurs
- ‚úÖ **Support system** through token holders (contractors and clients)
- ‚úÖ **Portfolio company investments** (600 companies over 5 years)
- ‚úÖ **Iterative growth model:** legal entity registration ‚Üí deployment ‚Üí marketing ‚Üí sales ‚Üí expansion
- ‚úÖ **Contributor recruitment** among local companies to accelerate the process

### 5-Year Plan (Q4 2025 - Q4 2030)

- Opening **100+ legal entities** in different countries
- **21,000+ entrepreneurs** in the first accelerator
- **Vision of the future:** 40% of all entrepreneurs worldwide by year 5 (133.2 million) ‚Äî see [forecasts and scenarios](for-investors.md#forecast-justification-from-conservative-10-to-optimistic-40-scenarios)
